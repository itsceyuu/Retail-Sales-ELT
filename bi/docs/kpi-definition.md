# KPI Definition — Retail Sales Dashboard

## 1. Total Sales
Deskripsi: Total nilai penjualan  
Formula: SUM(sales)

## 2. Total Profit
Deskripsi: Total keuntungan  
Formula: SUM(profit)

## 3. Profit Margin
Deskripsi: Persentase profit terhadap sales  
Formula: SUM(profit) / SUM(sales) * 100%

## 4. Total Orders
Deskripsi: Jumlah transaksi unik  
Formula: COUNT(DISTINCT order_id)
